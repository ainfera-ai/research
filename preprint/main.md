---
title: "Outcome-aware inference routing for AI agents"
author: "Hizrian Raz · Ainfera"
date: 2026
abstract: >
  Agents increasingly call many models. We route each call to the model likely
  to COMPLETE the task at the lowest all-in cost, learning a per-(model, task)
  quality signal (q_empirical) from outcome labels rather than API-layer status.
  On a reproducible synthetic benchmark, a learned policy completes more tasks
  at or below the agent's own baseline cost than static or single-model
  strategies, approaching the oracle. We describe the method, the judge protocol,
  and the exploration floor that keeps the policy from collapsing onto a local
  optimum. The operated policy and real corpus are closed; this paper publishes
  the method and a synthetic reproduction.
